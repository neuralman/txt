IFTTT - If This Then That is a free way to get all your online apps and devices talking to each other.

For example, you can setup a recipe to blink your Philips Hue light 3 times when your Uber ride arrives at your house. Or, setup a Weather Underground trigger to change your Philips Hue multi-color light automatically to blue if the temperature drops below 50°F or change it to red if the temperature rises above 70°F for example. You can also get a Wemo Smart Power Plug and activate whatever is plugged into it with IFTTT triggers!
